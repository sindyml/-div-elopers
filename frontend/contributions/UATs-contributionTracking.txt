Use case 1: Treasurer confirmation panel

User Story: "As a Treasurer, I want to confirm or flag each member's contribution, so that the group's payment records are accurate and up to date."

UAT 1.1: Treasurer confirms a pending payment
Given a Treasurer is logged in and viewing the Manage Contributions page (manage.html), and sees a contribution with status pending, 
when the Treasurer clicks the Confirm button next to that contribution, then the contribution status changes to confirmed in Firestore, 
the button disappears, and the status displays as "✅ Confirmed" on both the Treasurer's screen and the member's screen (my.html) without page refresh.

UAT 1.2: Treasurer flags a missed payment
Given a Treasurer is logged in and viewing the Manage Contributions page (manage.html), and sees a contribution with status pending,
when the Treasurer clicks the Miss button next to that contribution, then the contribution status changes to missed in Firestore,
the button disappears, and the status displays as "❌ Missed" on both the Treasurer's screen and the member's screen (my.html) without page refresh.

UAT 1.3: Treasurer cannot confirm already confirmed payment
Given a Treasurer is logged in and viewing the Manage Contributions page (manage.html), and sees a contribution with status confirmed/missed,
when the Treasurer looks at the Action column, then No Confirm or Miss buttons are displayed (only a dash "-"). 

UAT 1.4: Treasurer sees list of groups they manage
Gven a Treasurer is logged in and manages multiple stokvel groups, when the Treasurer navigates to the Manage Contributions screen,
then the Treasurer sees a list of all groups they are assigned to manage.

Use Case 2: Member views payout schedule

User Story: "As a Member, I want to view the payout schedule, so that I know when I will receive my payout."

UAT 2.1: Member sees payout schedule for a group
Given a Member is logged in and belongs to a stokvel group that has a payout schedule, when the Member clicks View Payout Schedule next to that group, 
then the Member sees a table showing the payout order, member names, payout dates, and amounts for that group.

UAT 2.2: Member sees message when no payout schedule exists
Given a Member is logged in and selects a group that has no payout schedule, when the Member clicks View Payout Schedule next to that group,
then the Member sees a message: "No payout schedule found for this group"

Use Case 3: Member views their contributions

User Story: "As a Member, I want to view my contribution history for each of my stokvel groups, so that I can track whether I am up to date with my payments."

UAT 3.1: Member sees their payment history
Given a Member is logged in and belongs to one or more stokvel groups, when the Member navigates to the Contribution Tracking screen, 
then the Member sees a list of their stokvel groups. For each group, they see a table showing the amount, date, and status of each payment they have made.


UAT 3.2: Member with no groups sees empty message
Given a Member is logged in but does not belong to any stokvel group, when the Member navigates to the Contribution Tracking screen,
then the Member sees a message: "You haven't joined any stokvel group yet".


UAT 3.3: Member views contributions for a specific group
Given a Member is logged in and belongs to multiple stokvel groups, when the Member clicks "View Contributions" on a specific group,
then the Member sees the payment history table for only that selected group.
